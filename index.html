INDEPENDENT ENGINEERING CONSULTING PRACTICE

High-reliability hardware systems for safety-critical applications.

Established in 2012, RGE Systems Inc. is an independent engineering consulting practice focused on the development of high-reliability hardware systems for safety-critical applications, meeting demanding robustness and regulatory requirements. The practice draws on extensive experience across aerospace, defense & security, life sciences, and other advanced technology sectors.
