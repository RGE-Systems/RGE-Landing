High-reliability hardware systems for safety-critical applications.

Established in 2012, RGE Systems Inc. is an independent engineering consulting practice focused on the development of high-reliability hardware systems for safety-critical applications, meeting demanding robustness and regulatory requirements.

The practice draws on extensive experience across aerospace, defense & security, life sciences, and other advanced technology sectors.

Core areas of work

  • Mechanical system architecture and design ownership, encompassing electronics packaging, opto-mechanical design, and ruggedization for harsh operating environments
  • Technology Readiness Level (TRL) progression, from early stages through commercialization
  • System-level integration, verification, and validation
  • Design reviews and risk assessments, including Design Failure Modes and Effects Analysis (DFMEA)
  • VAVE initiatives supporting cost, manufacturability, and overall system value
  • Prototyping and manufacturing support

How we engage

RGE Systems operates as a flexible platform for selective consulting, fractional technical engagement, technology development, and venture initiatives.
